Pancakes

Description

A breakfast staple, this page will go over creating fluffy pancakes to brighten your morning.

Ingredients

  • Pancake mix
  • Water
  • Cooking Oil
  • Butter
  • Maple Syrup

Steps

  1. Heat the pan
  2. Mix the pancake mix and water
  3. Add your cooking oil
  4. Add the batter to the pan
  5. When the bubbles begin to form flip your pancake
  6. After a few minutes remove the pancake from the pan
  7. Add butter and syrup on top then enjoy!
